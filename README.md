# Hotel Booking Analytics & Predictive Dashboard

## Overview
This project is an end-to-end data analytics and science initiative developed by **Ika, Kiki, and Aulia**. It focuses on analyzing hotel booking datasets to uncover patterns in cancellation behavior, revenue performance, and customer insights[cite: 1]. By combining interactive visualizations in **Tableau** with predictive modeling in **Python**, this project facilitates data-driven decision-making for hotel operations and risk mitigation.

---

## 1. Business & Data Understanding
In this initial phase, we defined the core challenges and objectives faced by **Hotel Group X**.
*   **Business Problem:** High booking cancellation rates are causing significant revenue leakage and operational inefficiencies.
*   **Data Source:** We utilized the public *Hotel Booking Demand Dataset* containing booking details for a city hotel and a resort hotel.
*   **Key Metrics (KPIs):** We identified essential indicators such as **Cancel Rate**, **Lead Time**, **Average Daily Rate (ADR)**, and **RevPAR** to measure success.
*   **Stakeholders:** Key beneficiaries include Hotel Management, Revenue Managers, and the Reservations Team.

## 2. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
Using **Python**, we performed a deep dive into the dataset to ensure data integrity and uncover early patterns.
*   **Data Cleaning:** Handled missing values, addressed outliers, and corrected data types for consistency.
*   **Feature Analysis:** Examined correlations between variables like lead time, deposit types, and previous cancellations.
*   **Insight Discovery:** Identified that long lead times and specific market segments correlate strongly with higher cancellation risks.

## 3. Tableau Visualization
We developed a suite of interactive dashboards to translate raw data into actionable business intelligence.
*   **Executive Overview:** High-level performance summary and macro trends for management.
*   **Cancellation Analysis:** Focused on risk factors to pinpoint where and why bookings are lost.
*   **Revenue Analysis:** Tracking financial performance and segment contributions.
*   **Customer Behavior:** Segmenting guests by loyalty, type, and booking preferences.
*   **Operational Excellence:** Monitoring room allocation and waiting list efficiency.

## 4. Machine Learning
To proactively address cancellations, we implemented a predictive modeling workflow.
*   **Preprocessing:** Conducted one-hot encoding for categorical data and feature scaling.
*   **Feature Engineering:** Developed new predictors such as total stay duration and guest history scores.
*   **Model Selection:** Evaluated classification algorithms including Logistic Regression and Random Forest to predict the likelihood of cancellation.
*   **Evaluation:** Optimized the model based on **Precision** (to avoid false alarms) and **Recall** (to catch as many cancellations as possible).
