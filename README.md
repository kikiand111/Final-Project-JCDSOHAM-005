# Hotel Booking Analytics & Predictive Dashboard

## Overview
This project is an end-to-end data analytics and science initiative developed by **Ika, Kiki, and Aulia**. It focuses on analyzing hotel booking datasets to uncover patterns in cancellation behavior, revenue performance, and customer insights. By combining interactive visualizations in **Tableau** with predictive modeling in **Python**, this project facilitates data-driven decision-making for hotel operations and risk mitigation.

## Business Context
**Hotel Group X** faces challenges with high booking cancellation rates, leading to significant revenue leakage and operational inefficiencies[cite: 1]. Understanding the underlying drivers—such as lead times and distribution channels—and predicting them through Machine Learning is essential for maintaining a competitive edge.

### Business Objectives
* **Minimize Revenue Leakage:** Reduce the financial impact of cancellations and no-shows[cite: 1].
* **Predictive Modeling:** Build Machine Learning models to classify high-risk reservations[cite: 1].
* **Operational Optimization:** Improve overbooking decisions and room allocation efficiency[cite: 1].
* **Strategic Pricing:** Optimize ADR (Average Daily Rate) based on historical demand and predicted trends[cite: 1].
* **Customer Loyalty:** Analyze behavior differences between repeat and first-time guests to improve retention[cite: 1].

## Machine Learning Integration
The project employs a robust Machine Learning workflow to predict the likelihood of cancellations:
* **Data Preprocessing:** Handling missing values, encoding categorical variables, and managing outliers[cite: 1].
* **Feature Engineering:** Developing key predictors such as total length of stay, lead time classification, and guest history[cite: 1].
* **Model Development:** Utilizing classification algorithms (e.g., Logistic Regression, Random Forest, or XGBoost) to predict booking status.
* **Evaluation Metrics:** Ensuring model reliability through Accuracy, Precision, Recall, and F1-Score analysis[cite: 1].

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
* **Cancel Rate:** Percentage of total bookings that were cancelled[cite: 1].
* **Lost Revenue:** Potential revenue lost due to cancellations[cite: 1].
* **Realized Revenue:** Actual revenue generated from completed stays[cite: 1].
* **ADR (Average Daily Rate):** Average income earned per occupied room[cite: 1].
* **Lead Time:** The duration between the booking date and arrival date[cite: 1].
* **Model Accuracy:** The success rate of the Machine Learning model in predicting cancellations.

## Dashboard Structure (Tableau)
The visual analysis is categorized into five specialized dashboards:
1. **Executive Overview:** High-level summary of performance metrics and macro trends[cite: 1].
2. **Cancellation Analysis:** Deep dive into risk factors and cancellation patterns[cite: 1].
3. **Revenue Analysis:** Tracking financial performance and segment contributions[cite: 1].
4. **Customer Behavior:** Analysis of loyalty, guest types, and booking characteristics[cite: 1].
5. **Operational Excellence:** Monitoring room allocation, waiting lists, and booking changes[cite: 1].
